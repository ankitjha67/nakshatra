## Subscriptions
- **Cancel anytime.** You can cancel auto-renewal from your account or by contacting
  `[SUPPORT EMAIL]`. Cancellation stops future renewals; you keep access until the end of
  the current paid cycle.
- **Refund window.** New subscriptions may be refunded within `[7]` days of the initial
  charge **if** you have not substantially used the paid features that cycle. After that,
  charges for the current cycle are generally non-refundable except where required by law
  or approved at our discretion.
- **Refunded subscriptions** are downgraded to the free tier and the cycle's token grant is
  reversed.

## Token top-up packs
- One-time top-up packs are **consumable**. **Tokens already consumed are non-refundable.**
- Unused top-up tokens may be refunded within `[7]` days of purchase at our discretion; the
  corresponding tokens are removed from your balance on refund.

## How to request a refund
1. Submit a request in-app (`POST /v1/refunds` with the payment id and a reason) or email
   `[SUPPORT EMAIL]` with your account email and payment reference.
2. We review for eligibility and fraud/abuse signals.
3. If approved, the refund is issued to your original payment method via Razorpay; your
   credits/tier are reversed automatically when the refund is processed.

## Processing time
Approved refunds are initiated within `[5-7]` business days; your bank/card issuer may take
additional time to post the credit.

## Fraud, abuse, and chargebacks
Refunds may be declined where we detect fraud or policy abuse. Initiating a chargeback
instead of contacting us may lead to suspension; please reach out first so we can help.
